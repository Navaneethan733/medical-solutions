Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an online consultation?

Simply click on the "Book Appointment" button, select your specialist, and choose "Online Consultation" as your visit type. You'll receive a secure link for your video call.

Is my medical data secure with STACKLY?

Absolutely. We use enterprise-grade end-to-encryption and comply with all international health data protection regulations (HIPAA/GDPR) to ensure your privacy.

Do you accept international insurance?

Yes, we partner with major global insurance providers. You can upload your insurance details during the booking process to check for coverage eligibility.

Can I get emergency care at any of your centers?

Our main hubs are equipped with 24/7 emergency departments. Smaller clinics provide urgent care during business hours. Please use the "Emergency Call" button for immediate assistance.
