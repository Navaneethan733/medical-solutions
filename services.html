FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first STACKLY appointment.

Are virtual consultations covered by insurance?

Yes, we work with over 40 major insurance providers. You can verify your coverage in real-time during the booking process. Out-of-pocket consultation fees start at just $25 for uninsured patients.

How quickly can I see a doctor online?

Typically within 10 to 30 minutes of booking for general consultations. Specialist appointments can often be scheduled for the same day or next day. For emergencies, our triage line connects you immediately.

Can I get a prescription through a virtual visit?

Absolutely. Our licensed physicians can issue electronic prescriptions for most medications during a virtual visit. Controlled substances may require an in-person evaluation per local regulations.

How does home sample collection for labs work?

Book a home collection slot online and a certified phlebotomist will visit your location at the chosen time. Samples are transported to our certified lab, and digital results are uploaded to your portal within 6 hours.

Is my health data private and secure?

Yes. All patient data is encrypted end-to-end and stored on HIPAA-compliant servers. We never sell or share your data with third parties without your explicit consent. Your health history is yours alone.

What should I do in a medical emergency?

Call our 24/7 emergency line immediately at 9876543210. Our dispatcher will coordinate ambulance dispatch and alert our trauma team. Do not attempt to drive to the hospital yourself in a life-threatening emergency.
