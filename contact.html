Quick Help

Common Questions

Before sending a message, check if your question has already been answered.

How do I book a virtual appointment?

You can book directly through our Services page or by creating an account on our patient portal. Our system allows you to choose a time slot that fits your schedule.

What insurance providers do you accept?

We accept over 40 major insurance providers, including Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, and UnitedHealthcare. Please verify your coverage in your profile settings or call our billing department.

How do I request a prescription refill?

Refills can be requested through the "My Medications" section of our app or by calling our Digital Pharmacy directly with your prescription ID.
