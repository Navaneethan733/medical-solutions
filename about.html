Our Story

Built on Care. Driven by Innovation.

Founded in 2010 by a group of leading physicians and tech pioneers, STACKLY was born from a simple belief: every person deserves access to world-class healthcare — without barriers, delays, or complexity.

Over the past 15 years, we have grown from a single outpatient clinic to a fully integrated health platform with 12 care centres, a digital pharmacy, state-of-the-art diagnostic labs, and a telemedicine network serving patients across the country.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

The principles that guide every decision, every treatment, and every interaction at STACKLY.

Our Mission

To make world-class healthcare accessible, affordable, and personalised for every individual — regardless of geography or circumstance — through the seamless integration of medicine and technology.

Our Vision

A world where distance, time, and economic barriers no longer stand between a person and the care they need. We envision healthcare that is proactive, predictive, and profoundly human.

Our Values

Integrity, compassion, and innovation. We hold ourselves to the highest standards of clinical ethics while fostering a culture of continuous learning and patient-first empathy.

Our Journey

Milestones That Define Us

From a single clinic to a nationwide health platform — every step has been driven by patient impact.

2010

Founded

STACKLY opens its first outpatient clinic in New York, with a founding team of 8 physicians and a mission to transform patient care.

2013

Diagnostics Lab Launched

We opened our flagship in-house diagnostics centre, cutting average report turnaround time from 48 hours to under 6 hours.

2016

Digital Pharmacy

Launch of the STACKLY Digital Pharmacy, enabling doorstep delivery of prescriptions for the first time in our network.

2019

JCI Accreditation

STACKLY becomes one of the first independent health networks in the region to achieve full Joint Commission International accreditation.

2021

Telemedicine Platform

Rapid expansion of our 24/7 teleconsultation service during the pandemic, reaching over 50,000 virtual visits in the first year alone.

2025

AI-Powered Diagnostics

Integration of AI-assisted imaging and pathology analysis across all lab centres, improving diagnostic accuracy by over 30%.
